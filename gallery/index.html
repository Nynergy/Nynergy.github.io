Gallery

Sometimes I like to mess around with digital images and other visual media. This is where you can find all those collections.

Genius Loci
Genius Loci is a study in layering, melding, and abstracting normal images in order to convey the "spirit" of a place or landscape. This collection is a bit smaller, but feels far more uniform and polished among each piece.
View Gallery
Strange Negatives
Strange Negatives is made up of images I collected mostly from camera rolls, and edited with GIMP. The main focus of my editing was on color composition, video degradation, and other similarly "cyber-trashy" aesthetics.
