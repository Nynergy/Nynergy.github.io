2Xth Century Genuflectors

In X amount in time as flowing by in spurious memories. These neurons firing rails of light. Corrupted state, reset. These are the documents. Your correspondence is appreciated. Borealis songs skipping school girls. Screwed in the metal ground. Here where there is somewhere other before remembering in scraps of flaming photographs. The gun barrels pointed fingers accused. Glass people glass churches prone under the bridge gazers. Jumping. Smaller crucifixes growing. Mark the spreadsheets hanging over after lifters raising limbs from earth. Suits sparkle dull boys parking outside the scene. We live here drinking tumbling what was that. Quick dry facade painted over.

Come listen in. Speaking tongues and teeth. These people are not to be trusted. They are within and without us.
