Writing

See More  

I've written poetry and prose for many years, far longer than I've been making music. My poetry typically focuses on abstract emotions, pulling a lot of imagery from nature and the environment, even dreams.


Some of these pieces I felt were good enough to publish, and so I've edited, designed, typeset, and self-published a number of volumes through Amazon. Free PDF downloads of the collections are also available on the site.
