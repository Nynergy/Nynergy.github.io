# [Blog] Netrunner

- Created:  June 16, 2026
- Status:   OPEN

I want to talk about what makes Netrunner such a special game to me. The
mechanics, the community, the people I've met, the joys of getting to mentor and
foster a group of people who also enjoy this wonderful game.

Mechanics:
- Asymmetrical
- Hidden information
- Not just a dude smasher
- Deckbuilding

Community:
- Friendly
- Inclusive
- Fan-driven (Null Signal Games)
- Low barrier to entry (Print-and-Play, proxy-friendly)

People:
- Longtime friends
- Sharing joy of the game
- Stewarding a space for others
- Growing as a player, and as a person
