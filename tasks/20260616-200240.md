# [Blog] Mahjong Philosophy

- Created:  June 16, 2026
- Status:   OPEN

Riichi mahjong is a very special game to me, and it's changed how I view gaming
as a means to connect with those around me.

I'd love to talk about my mahjong philosophy, which is heavily inspired by the
talk Tsuchida Koshou gave at Riichi Nomi NYC about playing mahjong with dignity.
I'd also like to relate a lot of my thoughts to Close to the Edge by Yes,
specifically the refrain of "I get up, I get down".
