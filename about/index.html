About Me

My name is Ben Buchanan, and I made the stuff you're looking at on this site. Sometimes I write, sometimes I make music, sometimes I create art. Perhaps I might spend a week straight programming some tool or interface. Or maybe I'll spend far too much time ignoring this site and playing video games.

I also have a day job where I type on a keyboard and sit in a nice office chair. Not every day is a creative one. And that's okay.

I hope you enjoy the stuff I make, or find something interesting on the site. Thanks for visiting, and be well.

- Ben
