About

What is Lexica Chromatica?

Lexica Chromatica is the personal website of Ben Buchanan. That's me. Hello, I am writing this, I am Ben. I made this website to serve as an archive of things I find interesting, or for projects, or other assorted thoughts I'm having. You can find various poems that I upload and links to my published collections of poetry on the aptly-named "Poetry" page. Some scattered (and often unfinished) prose pieces can be found on the "Prose" page. My half-baked, malformed, and otherwise insubstantial music projects can be found on the "Music" page. I sometimes do a regular Internet thing and write blog posts, which you can find on the "Blog" page. If the other excrutiatingly monotonous creative projects on the site weren't enough for you, feel free to peruse my digital image galleries on the "Gallery" page. For more information about Lexica Chromatica and myself, head over to the "About" page. If you're not currently on the "About" page, then please tell me how you're reading this, I cannot fathom how it would be possible outside of a webscraping application. As you can tell from the long-winded and frankly unnecessary explanation above, I like to keep things simple here. Pages are appropriately named and easy to navigate to/from. There's a navbar at the top of the page, use it well. I do not place advertisements on my website - outside of foisting my books and other projects on you, of course. I try to make things look nice without creating too much clutter to sift through. Hopefully you appreciate the site in the same way I have come to. If you have any comments, tips, questions, etc, then send me an email with the contact form at the bottom of this page.

Who is Ben Buchanan?

I'm a writer, author, poet, musician, artist, whatever the hell people are calling things these days. I do stuff in my spare time and I have a lot of fun with it. You can see some of the fruits of my work on this very website, be it my poetry, prose, music, or image galleries. I write blog posts, too, but that's not very creative. Mostly just me spouting endless inane nonsense until I tire of writing anything more. Sometimes I just want to see that "Recent Posts" table shift down a bit, you know what I mean? You can see some older poems I've written (most of which are reaching the ripe old age of "cringe-inducing" at this point) by going to my Instagram. My poetry Instagram, that is. I have a separate account for my personal stuff, but you're more than welcome to check that out as well if you feel so inclined. Links for both are down below.

For real, though, who is this guy?

So you weren't fooled by all that stuff about being a creative, huh? That's fair. In reality, I'm a developer at a local campus IT department, making little web applications and tools for people. And if that wasn't enough for you, I also make my own open source projects on Github. You can find a link to my Github page below. I guess if you want an actual answer for what I do, I'm a full-time code monkey, if you will. And it's that primate knowledge that's allowed me to make this website, so at least I have something to show for it. Also I have a Twitter, I guess? You kids and your fancy social media. Much like my Instagram, I don't plan to post too much; I really just use it to follow and keep up with friends and the like. Feel free to contact me there if you want.
