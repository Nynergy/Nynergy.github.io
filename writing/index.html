I started writing poetry around 2014, but it wasn't until 2018 that I seriously began considering publishing some of my material in volumes. I downloaded a typesetting program and put together my first collection of original poetry in 2019, titled Babylon Effect, self-publishing it through KDP (Amazon).


Since then, I've taught myself typesetting with LaTeX on the command line, published two other volumes of poetry (Another Flow in 2020, and Drift Illogical in 2021), and even released a second edition of Babylon Effect with updated styling and extra content.


Links to paperbacks on Amazon are available, as well as free PDF downloads of each manuscript.
