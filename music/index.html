Maps of Low Fidelity

Unknown Coordinates (2022)

Unknown Coordinates, and Maps of Low Fidelity in general, is my attempt at stripping back some of my sound, and focusing on natural, textural, calming songs.

This style is very much inspired by the late Lindsheaven Virtual Plaza, specifically his Rainforest Hill series, which I adore and highly recommend.

Ben Buchanan

Inner Weather (2021)

Inner Weather was the first album where I experimented with modular synthesis using VCV Rack. I incorporated samples, hooked up my cheap MIDI controller, and threw compositional ideas at the wall until something stuck.

What I came away with was this collection of ambient electronic pieces focusing on sample manipulation with granular synthesis, as well as textural experimentation.

Despite some of the lackluster production and performances, I'm still quite proud of what I made on this project. It made me fall in love with modular, and in the process I felt I could construct very fluid, emotive songs that fit into my abstract creative process.

Neuromelter

This Isolation is in My DNA (2018)

This Isolation is in My DNA, and Neuromelter as a whole, was kind of a failed experiment. At the time I had wanted to try my hand at a more direct, abrasive, hardcore electronic sound, but ended up bleeding into the melodic ambient realm much of the time.

While there are some interesting ideas present, I haven't touched Neuromelter in years, and don't plan to come back to it anytime soon.
