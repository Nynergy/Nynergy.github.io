Inner Weather

Inner Weather // 2021

View on Bandcamp
Inner Weather is my latest collection of ambient electronic tracks. Unlike my last release, A Dream of Shapes Returning, this album doesn't focus on tape loops - in fact, it doesn't use any at all. Instead, I decided to experiment with modular synthesis using VCV Rack, and these are the songs that came of it.

This album features some of my most direct tracks since my work under the Neuromelter name. However, it hasn't lost that meditative, somewhat delicate ambient style I cultivated on my last record. I think this variety adds a bit more character to the album, and keeps it from being too one-note. Just like A Dream of Shapes Returning, these tracks were all recorded and performed live, without the careful sequencing and automation of a DAW, and can be downloaded for pay-what-you-want on Bandcamp.

If you'd like to hear Inner Weather, you can listen to a track using the embedded player above, or by visiting my Bandcamp page using the button beside it. I hope you come to enjoy and appreciate this album in the same way I have while making it.
