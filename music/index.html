Music

I've made music under a couple different names. Here are links and files for all of it.

Ben Buchanan

A Dream of Shapes Returning

The music I put under my actual name is ambient music made with homemade cassette tape loops. It's meant to be more low-key than my other projects, featuring more lofi production, and recorded from live sessions in front of my MIDI controller. You can download A Dream of Shapes Returning on bandcamp for pay-what-you-want.

Listen to A Dream of Shapes Returning on Bandcamp

Eastern Experiment // Red Miles

Before I used tape loops, I created a double-LP of music as a test of my compositional skills and sampling ability. That double-LP, called Eastern Experiment // Red Miles, was never published to my bandcamp page. You can download the archive using the link below.

Download Eastern Experiment // Red Miles

Neuromelter

This Isolation is In My DNA

Neuromelter was originally meant to be an outlet for more hardcore electronic production, with cleaner, more experimental instrumentation. However, it also ended up bleeding a bit into the ambient realm. Just like my other releases, you can download This Isolation is In My DNA on bandcamp for pay-what-you-want.
